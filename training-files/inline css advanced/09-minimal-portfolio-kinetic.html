About

I believe design is an act of translation — taking complex ideas and making them impossible to misread.

Twelve years ago I started with letterpress printing in Ibadan. Today I work at the scale of complete visual identities, but the same question drives every project: what is the clearest, most honest way to say this?

I have worked for publishers, distilleries, financial institutions, and cultural organisations. The sector changes; the discipline doesn't.
